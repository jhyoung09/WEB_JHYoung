Cisco Unified Communication Manager Upgrade

Home / Portfolio / Cisco Unified Communication Manager Upgrade

Project Details

As part of the Voice Engineering team and the secondary Subject Matter Expert for the Cisco Unified Communications Manager environment, I helped plan and execute the upgrade of CUCM 8.6 to CUCM 10.5. This upgrade allowed the team to restructure our server infrastructure and resize the environment to provide adequate resources and disaster recovery. With our planning and testing, the team was able to upgrade and resize the entire environment with no downtime.
